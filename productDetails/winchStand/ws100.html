WS 100 WINCH-STAND

PLS WINCH STAND WS-100 is a reliable and sturdy option for your lifting and elevation needs. Made of high-quality steel, it has a maximum capacity of 110kgs and can reach a height of up to 4m. It comes in a sleek black color and is made in India with a one-year manufacturer warranty.

One of the standout features of this stand is its ability to open its legs in three different positions, allowing for customizable elevation and working area options. Additionally, its special adjustment system ensures a smooth rise of the different sections without any oscillation between the bars, making it safe and reliable to use.

Another feature that adds to the overall durability and safety of this stand is its long-life stainless safety pins. These pins ensure that the stand stays securely in place and minimizes any risk of accidents or injury.

Overall, the PLS WINCH STAND WS-100 is a top-of-the-line option for anyone in need of a reliable, sturdy, and safe lifting and elevation stand. Whether you're using it for personal or professional purposes, this stand is sure to meet all your needs and exceed your expectations.

Product Specification

  • Maximum Height = 4m
  • Weight of stand = 17.5kgs
  • Maximum Capacity = 110kgs
  • Made of steel
  • Available color: Black
  • Its legs can be opened in 3 positions depending on the needs of elevation and working area
  • It has a special adjustment system that allows a smooth rise of the different sections avoiding the oscillation between the bars.
  • Long life stainless safety pins.
  • 1 year Manufacturer warranty
  • Made in india

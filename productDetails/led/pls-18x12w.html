PLS 18x12W

The light source of this product is composed of 18X12 ultra bright TX LED's, which provide a powerful and reliable source of light for a variety of lighting applications. The lens angle of the product is 25o, which helps to ensure that the light is directed where it is needed most. The product has 8 DMX channel modes, which allows for a wide range of control options.

The light has a range of functions, including color mix, automatic color change, built-in programs, and strobe effect with adjustable speed. The light can also be sound-controlled via a built-in microphone, with adjustable sound sensitivity. The LED display makes it easy to control the light, and the product also features overheating protection for added safety. The voltage of the light is 100-220V,50/60hz, and it has a power of 180W. The product has an IP rating of IP20, making it suitable for indoor use only. The product is made in India and has certifications such as CE, ISO, and ROHS. The weight of the product is 5.62KGS, and its dimensions are 320X310X230MM. Overall, this product is a reliable and versatile lighting option that can be used in a wide range of settings, from concerts and events to theatrical productions and more.

PLS PROFILE 300W

The PLS LED FOLLOW -300 is a powerful and versatile lighting fixture that is perfect for a wide range of applications. With a power supply of AC100-240V and a power consumption of 500W, this fixture packs a punch with its 300W LED light source. The lifetime of the LED is an impressive 5000 hours, making this fixture a reliable and long-lasting investment.

The PLS LED FOLLOW -300 offers a range of color temperatures built-in, including 7500K, 4600K, 3200K, and atomization. Its beam angle can be adjusted from 1-9°, allowing you to achieve precise lighting effects. It also features manual control panel and DMX-512 control modes, with 4 DMX channels available. The linear dimming function ranges from 0-100%, giving you complete control over the brightness of the fixture.

Product Specification

  • Power supply: AC100-240V.50Hz-60Hz
  • Power consumption: 500W
  • Light source: 300W LED
  • Lifetime: 5000 hours
  • Color temperature: built-in 7500K, 4600K, 3200K, atomization
  • Beam Angle: 1-9°
  • Control Mode: Manual Control Panel, DMX-512
  • DMX Channels: 4CHS
  • Dimmer: 0-100% Linear Dimming
  • Iris: Linear Zoom in & Zoom Out

COB BAR 200W INDOOR

This product is a lighting fixture that is designed for indoor use, with a weight of only 3kgs and dimensions of 300x220x320 mm, making it a compact and easy-to-use product. The LED light source is a 1pcs 200-watt COB LED lamp (WW/CW), which provides a bright and powerful light output. One of the key features of this product is that it is flicker-free, ensuring that there is no visible flicker when used for video or film purposes.

The product also comes with built-in programs and can be controlled in a variety of ways, including auto, master/slave, DMX512, software (connect to computer), and sound control. With a DMX channel of 2 channels, users have more control over the lighting effects. The dimmer is also 100% linearly adjustable, providing more precise control over the brightness of the light. Additionally, the product has a strobe setting of 0~25F.P.S and a voltage range of AC100-240V 50/60Hz. The product has an IP rating of IP20, meaning that it is not suitable for outdoor use and should only be used in dry and indoor environments. Overall, this lighting fixture is a versatile and powerful product that can be used for a variety of applications, including concerts, events, and theatrical performances.

Product Specification

  • Flicker free
  • Built-in programs
  • LED Light Source: 1pcs 200-watt COB LED lamp (WW/CW)
  • Lamp average life time: 50,000 hours
  • DMX Channel: 2 channels
  • Control Mode: Auto, Master/Slave, DMX512, software (connect to computer), sound control
  • Dimmer: 100% linearly adjustable
  • Strobe: 0~25F.P. S
  • Voltage: AC100-240V 50/60Hz
  • Working Environment: indoor -20°C- 40°C
  • IP Rating: IP20
  • Dimensions: 300x220x320 mm

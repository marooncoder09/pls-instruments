LED MAGIC PAR 54x03 OutDoor

This lighting fixture features 54PCS 3W CREE LED's that are capable of producing a range of colors with precision and brightness. The lens angle of 25 degrees allows for a versatile spread of light, making it suitable for a range of lighting applications. With 8 DMX channel mode, users have the option to control various aspects of the fixture's behavior, including color mixing, automatic color change, built-in programs, and strobe effects. Additionally, the sound-controlled built-in microphone and adjustable sound sensitivity make it an ideal fixture for events that require dynamic lighting changes based on the music or ambiance.

With a voltage range of 100-220V and 50/60Hz, this fixture is suitable for use in various countries worldwide. The IP65 rating makes it suitable for both indoor and outdoor use, while the overheating protection ensures the safety of the fixture and the users. Additionally, the certifications of CE, ISO, and ROHS add to the safety and reliability of the product. With a weight of 5.62KG, this lighting fixture is portable and easy to install. The combination of functionality and safety makes this an ideal fixture for events and installations that require high-quality, dynamic lighting.

Product Specification

  • Light source: 54PCS 3W LEDs (R12 G14 B14 W14) CREE LED’S
  • Lens angle: 25º
  • Channel mode: 8 DMX channel mode
  • 0-100% linear dimmer
  • Function: colour mix, automatic colour change, built-in program and strobe effect with adjustable speed
  • Sound controlled via built-in microphone
  • Adjustable sound sensitivity
  • LED Display, easy to control
  • Control: auto, sound active, DMX512, software (connect to computer)
  • Overheating protection
  • IP rating: IP65
  • Certifications: CE, ISO, ROHS

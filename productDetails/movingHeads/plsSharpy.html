PLS BEAM 295W

Introducing our high-performance moving head stage light, designed for professional use in live events, concerts, theaters, and other entertainment venues. This powerful light is packed with features that enable it to create stunning visual effects and enhance the overall atmosphere of any performance.

With its 295W light source and 155mm out of light lens diameter, this stage light is capable of delivering bright, clear and vibrant colors that will captivate your audience. The expected life of the lamp bulbs is 2000 hours, ensuring that the product lasts for an extended period of time.

This moving head stage light comes with a color temperature of 7800K and a voltage range of AC100-240V.50/60HZ, making it easy to use in any location. It also has a compact size of 55.5x40.5x41cm and a net weight of 14.86kgs, making it easy to transport and install.

With its advanced optical system, this stage light ensures clear image quality, while the 2° beam angle creates a focused beam of light that can be directed precisely where needed. The control panel is user-friendly with an LCD color panel and 5 buttons displaying a Chinese and English interface. You can control it using DMX512, DM, self-propelled, master-slave, voice control functions with 16 channels.

The X-axis/Y-axis of this stage light has 16-bit precision scanning, and a photoelectric reset system is used for horizontal and vertical direction of X-axis 540° and Y-axis 270°. Accidental collision error can be automatically retrieved and reset.

The color disc has one fixed color disc, which can achieve half-color, panchromatic, single and double color gradient, and slow motions in positive and negative directions. The fixed pattern plate has ten kinds of metal patterns +3 kinds of glass patterns. It also has a dual prism equipped with a bi-directionally rotated and stacked 8 prism and a 16+8 prism.

The stroboscopic frequency is as high as 1-14 times/second, and the stroboscopic frequency speed can be adjusted arbitrarily with smooth dimming between 0% and 100%. This stage light also has an independent atomizing effect and independent seven-color sheets.

The dust-proof and oil-proof design ensures a long-lasting service life, while the electronic ballast, bubble point system, and intelligent fan speed control provide precise and efficient control over the light output. You can also use DMX to connect and update the software.

This moving head stage light can operate in a maximum tolerable ambient temperature of 45°C, maximum tolerable surface temperature of 65°C, and a lowest acceptable operating temperature of -5°C. It is a versatile and reliable lighting solution for your entertainment needs.
